Privacy Policy

AI Palm Reading App

Last Updated: February 8, 2026
TL;DR: Your privacy matters. All palm readings are stored locally on your device. We only use Google's Gemini AI to analyze your palm images - no other third parties have access to your data.

1. Introduction

Welcome to AI Palm Reading. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our mobile application. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring transparency about our data practices.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Palm Images

  • What: Photos of your palm captured through the app's camera
  • How: You voluntarily capture these images using our in-app camera
  • Storage: Stored locally on your device only
  • Purpose: To provide AI-powered palm reading analysis

2.2 Reading Data

  • AI-generated palm reading text and insights
  • Hand information (left/right, dominant/non-dominant)
  • Reading timestamps
  • Chat conversation history with AI

2.3 Technical Data

  • App version and device type (for debugging)
  • Error logs (anonymous, for app improvement)

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 AI Analysis

When you capture a palm image, we send it to Google's Gemini AI API for analysis. This is necessary to provide you with palm reading insights. Google processes the image according to their privacy policy and terms of service.

3.2 Local Storage

All readings, images, and chat histories are stored locally on your device using AsyncStorage. We do not upload this data to any external servers (except the Gemini API for analysis purposes).

3.3 App Improvement

Anonymous usage statistics and error reports may be collected to improve app performance and fix bugs. No personal data or palm images are included in these reports.

4. Data Sharing and Third Parties

4.1 Google Gemini AI

We use Google's Gemini AI service to analyze palm images. When you request a reading:

  • Your palm image is sent to Google's Gemini API
  • Google processes the image to generate insights
  • The analysis is returned to you and stored on your device
  • Google's use of this data is governed by their Privacy Policy

4.2 No Other Third Parties

We do not share your data with any other third parties, advertisers, or data brokers. Your palm images and readings remain private.

5. Data Retention and Deletion

5.1 Local Data

  • All data is stored on your device until you delete it
  • You can delete individual readings at any time
  • You can clear all data through the Profile screen
  • Uninstalling the app removes all local data

5.2 Gemini API

Google may temporarily cache images sent to their API. Refer to Google's privacy policy for details on their data retention practices.

6. Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data:

  • All data stored locally on your device (protected by device encryption)
  • API communications use HTTPS encryption
  • No passwords or accounts required (no authentication data stored)
  • No cloud backup of palm images or readings

7. Your Rights and Choices

7.1 Access and Control

  • View: See all your saved readings in the History screen
  • Delete: Remove individual readings or all data
  • Control: Choose when to capture palm images

7.2 Camera Permissions

  • The app requests camera permission to capture palm photos
  • You can deny or revoke this permission at any time
  • Without camera access, you cannot capture new readings

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children. If you believe a child has used our app, please contact us.

9. International Users

This app can be used worldwide. Your data is stored on your local device. When using Gemini AI, your images may be processed on Google's servers, which may be located in different countries.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes by updating the "Last Updated" date. Continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

11. Disclaimer

Important: AI Palm Reading is for entertainment and self-reflection purposes only. It does not predict the future, provide medical advice, or make life decisions for you. Readings are AI-generated interpretations and should not be taken as factual or professional advice.

12. Contact Us

Questions About Privacy?
